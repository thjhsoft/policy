用户隐私政策

生效日期：2025年6月1日  |  版本：1.0
感谢您选择《纸机空战 (Paper Plane Battle)》。我们非常重视您的隐私，本政策将说明我们如何收集、使用和保护您的个人信息。使用本应用即表示您同意本政策。

一、收集的信息

我们可能收集以下信息：

  • 设备信息：设备型号、操作系统版本、广告 ID（AAID）等，用于广告投放与分析。
  • 游戏数据：您的游戏进度、设置选项、购买记录（存储在本地设备）。
  • 广告数据：由 Google AdMob 收集的广告交互数据（点击、曝光等）。
  • 崩溃日志：用于改善应用稳定性的匿名崩溃报告。

我们不会主动收集您的姓名、电话号码、邮箱地址或银行信息。

二、信息使用目的

  • 展示个性化或非个性化广告（通过 Google AdMob）。
  • 改善游戏体验，修复漏洞，分析使用情况。
  • 遵守法律法规要求。

三、广告与第三方服务

本应用使用 Google AdMob 展示广告。AdMob 可能使用 Cookie 或广告标识符来为您提供相关广告。您可参阅 Google 的隐私政策了解详情：

🔗 https://policies.google.com/privacy

如您位于欧洲经济区（EEA）或英国，我们将在展示个性化广告前通过 Google UMP（用户消息平台）征询您的同意。您可随时在设置中撤回同意。

四、数据存储与安全

  • 游戏进度仅存储在您的本地设备，不上传至我们的服务器。
  • 广告数据由 Google 按照其安全标准处理。
  • 我们采取合理的技术措施保护您的信息安全。

五、儿童隐私

本应用面向 13 岁及以上用户。我们不会故意收集 13 岁以下儿童的个人信息。如发现儿童使用本应用，请家长联系我们，我们将立即处理。

六、您的权利

  • 访问 / 更正：您可随时查看或修改本地存储的游戏数据（通过重置存档功能）。
  • 撤回同意：可在系统设置中撤回广告个性化同意（仅限 EEA 用户）。
  • 删除数据：卸载应用即可删除本地所有游戏数据。
  • 退出广告个性化：可在 Android 系统设置 → 隐私 → 广告中重置广告 ID。

七、政策更新

我们保留更新本隐私政策的权利。修订后的政策将在应用内公布，重大变更将通过更新日期告知。继续使用本应用视为接受新政策。

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 1, 2025  |  Version: 1.0
Thank you for playing Paper Plane Battle (纸机空战). We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information. By using the app, you agree to this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Device Info: Device model, OS version, advertising ID (AAID) – used for ads and analytics.
  • Game Data: Your progress, settings, and in-app choices (stored locally on device only).
  • Ad Data: Ad interaction data (clicks, impressions) collected by Google AdMob.
  • Crash Logs: Anonymous crash reports used to improve stability.

We do not collect your name, phone number, email address, or payment details.

2. How We Use Information

  • To display personalized or non-personalized ads (via Google AdMob).
  • To improve gameplay, fix bugs, and analyze usage patterns.
  • To comply with applicable laws and regulations.

3. Advertising & Third-Party Services

This app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may use cookies or advertising identifiers to serve relevant ads. Please see Google's Privacy Policy:

🔗 https://policies.google.com/privacy

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA) or UK, we will request your consent via Google UMP (User Messaging Platform) before serving personalized ads. You may withdraw consent at any time in Settings.

4. Data Storage & Security

  • Game progress is stored locally on your device only – not on our servers.
  • Ad data is processed by Google under their security standards.
  • We employ reasonable technical measures to protect your information.

5. Children's Privacy

This app is intended for users 13 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

6. Your Rights

  • Access / Correct: View or reset your local game data via the in-game "Reset Progress" feature.
  • Withdraw Consent: EEA/UK users may withdraw ad-personalization consent in Settings.
  • Delete Data: Uninstalling the app removes all local game data.
  • Opt Out of Ad Personalization: Go to Android Settings → Privacy → Ads → Reset Advertising ID.

7. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated policies will be published within the app. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
