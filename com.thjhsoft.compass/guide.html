Q: What if the compass is inaccurate?

A: Try the following:

  • Move phone in figure-8 pattern to calibrate
  • Stay away from magnetic objects and electronic devices
  • Restart the app
  • Check if phone sensors are working properly

Q: Why does GPS show "Waiting for GPS"?

A: Possible reasons:

  • Location permission not granted
  • GPS not enabled
  • Indoors or in area with weak signal
  • First fix takes time

A: Tap the menu button (three horizontal lines icon) in the top right corner to view user guide and privacy policy.
