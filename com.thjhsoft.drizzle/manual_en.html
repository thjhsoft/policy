User Manual

Welcome to Drizzle, your personal relaxation and focus companion.

1. Create Your Scene

Tap the "+" button to start creating a unique soundscape. Combine atmosphere tracks (like Forest or Rain) with various layers (like Thunder or Wind).

2. Adjust Volume

Use the sliders to find the perfect balance for each layer. You can also control the master volume for the entire scene.

3. Set a Timer

Going to sleep? Set a sleep timer to automatically stop the playback after a certain period.

4. AdMob Advertising

To keep Drizzle free for everyone, we display minimal advertisements using Google AdMob. These ads help support the development and maintenance of the app.
