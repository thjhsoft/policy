Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how Drizzle handles your information.

1. Data Collection

Drizzle is designed to work offline. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal identifiable information (PII) to our servers.

2. App Usage

All your created scenes and settings are stored locally on your device. We do not have access to your personal audio preferences or usage patterns.

3. Advertising (AdMob)

Drizzle uses Google AdMob for advertising. Google may use advertising identifiers from your device to serve personalized ads. These identifiers do not contain your name or email. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for more information on how they handle data.

4. Age Restriction

Drizzle is suitable for all ages. There are no age restrictions for using the application.
