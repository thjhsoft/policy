概述

本隐私协议适用于由 thjhsoft 开发的移动应用程序「楚汉争霸：巅峰」（以下简称"本应用"）。我们非常重视您的隐私权，本协议说明我们如何收集、使用和保护您的信息。

本应用为单机象棋程序，我们不主动收集任何个人身份信息。为维持应用免费运营，应用内集成了 Google AdMob 广告服务，该服务可能收集设备相关信息以投放个性化广告。

一、信息收集

我们（开发者）不会主动收集以下信息：

  • 姓名、电话、电子邮件等个人身份信息
  • 精确位置信息

  • 游戏设置（音效、震动等偏好）
  • 对局历史记录
  • 残局完成进度

上述数据仅存储于设备本地，不会上传至任何服务器。

  • 广告互动数据（展示、点击等）

二、权限说明

本应用仅申请以下设备权限：

  • 震动（VIBRATE）：用于落子时的触觉反馈，不涉及任何数据访问。

本应用使用 Google AdMob 提供应用内广告，以支持本应用的免费运营。AdMob 由 Google LLC 运营，其隐私政策适用于广告数据的收集与处理。

Google 隐私政策：https://policies.google.com/privacy

Overview

This Privacy Policy applies to the mobile application "XiangQi Pinnacle" (hereinafter "the App") developed by thjhsoft. We respect your privacy and this policy explains how we handle your information.

This App is a standalone XiangQi game. We do not actively collect any personally identifiable information. To keep the App free, we integrate Google AdMob, which may collect device-related information to serve personalized advertisements.

1. Information Collection

We (the developer) do not actively collect any of the following:

  • Personal identity information (name, phone number, email, etc.)
  • Precise location data
  • Data from sensitive permissions (contacts, camera, microphone, etc.)

The following data is stored locally on your device for gameplay functionality:

  • Game settings (sound, vibration preferences)
  • Game history records
  • Endgame puzzle completion progress

This data is stored only on your device and is never uploaded to any server.

⚠️ The integrated Google AdMob service may collect the following (processed by Google):

  • Advertising ID
  • Device model, OS version, and other device information
  • IP address and approximate geographic location
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks, etc.)

2. Permissions

This App requests only the following device permission:

  • Vibration (VIBRATE): Used for haptic feedback when moving pieces. No data is accessed.

3. Third-Party Advertising (Google AdMob)

This App uses Google AdMob to display in-app advertisements to support the free availability of the App. AdMob is operated by Google LLC, and its privacy policy governs the collection and use of advertising data.

AdMob may serve ads in the following ways:

  • Personalized ads: Based on your interests and usage behavior (with your consent)
  • Non-personalized ads: Contextual only, without personal tracking

You can manage your ad preferences by:

  • Going to Settings → Privacy → Ads on your device to reset or limit your Advertising ID
  • Visiting https://adssettings.google.com to manage Google ad personalization

Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

AdMob Terms of Service: https://developers.google.com/admob/terms

4. Children's Privacy

This App is suitable for users of all ages. We do not actively collect personal information from children. Since the App integrates AdMob, parents or guardians can enable child protection modes or restrict ad tracking in device settings to ensure children's privacy.

5. Data Security

All game data is stored locally on your device and protected by the device's system-level security. We have no access to any data on your device.

6. Policy Updates

If this policy changes, we will notify users in the app update release notes. Significant changes will be communicated via an in-app notification.
