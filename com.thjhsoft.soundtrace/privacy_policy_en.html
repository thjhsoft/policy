Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to SoundTrace Voice Recorder ("we," "our," or "the App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our voice recording application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Audio Recordings: The App records and stores audio files locally on your device. We do not upload or transmit your recordings to our servers.
  • Recording Metadata: Information such as recording date, duration, file name, and custom notes you add to recordings.
  • App Settings: Your preferences including recording format, sample rate, theme mode, and other customization options.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, and app version for troubleshooting purposes.
  • Usage Data: Anonymous usage statistics to help improve app performance and features.

3. Advertising

AdMob Advertising

This app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use the following information:

  • Device advertising ID
  • IP address
  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • App usage data
  • Location data (if you grant location permissions)

AdMob uses this information to provide personalized advertisements. You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device's ad settings:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization

For more information about how Google uses data, please visit: Google Privacy Policy

For AdMob's privacy practices: AdMob Privacy

4. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide core app functionality (recording, playback, editing)
  • To save your preferences and settings
  • To improve app performance and user experience
  • To display relevant advertisements through AdMob
  • To troubleshoot technical issues

5. Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: All your audio recordings are stored locally on your device. We do not have access to your recordings.
  • No Cloud Backup: We do not automatically upload your recordings to any cloud service.
  • Security: Your recordings are protected by your device's security features (screen lock, encryption).

6. Permissions

The App requires the following permissions:

  • Microphone: Required to record audio
  • Storage: Required to save and access recordings on your device
  • Internet: Required to display advertisements

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to third parties, except:

  • AdMob: As described in Section 3, for advertising purposes
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Delete your recordings at any time through the app
  • Uninstall the app to remove all local data
  • Opt out of personalized advertising
  • Request information about data we collect

9. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

11. Third-Party Services

This app uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob: For displaying advertisements
  • FFmpeg: For audio processing (runs locally, no data transmission)
