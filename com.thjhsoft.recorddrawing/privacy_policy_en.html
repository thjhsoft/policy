Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

Important Notice: Draw Replay (hereinafter referred to as "the App") takes your privacy very seriously. The App does not collect, upload, or share any of your personal information or drawing data. All data is stored locally on your device.

1. Data Collection

The App does NOT collect any of the following information:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone, etc.)
  • Device identification information (IMEI, MAC address, etc.)
  • Location information
  • Your drawing content
  • Usage behavior data
  • Any other personal data

2. Data Storage

All your drawings, recorded drawing processes, and settings created in the App:

  • Are stored only on your device's local storage
  • Are not uploaded to any servers
  • Are not shared with third parties
  • Are completely controlled and managed by you

3. Permission Usage

The App may request the following permissions, used only for basic functions:

  • Storage Permission: Used to save and read your drawings to device gallery
  • Photo Library Access: Used to select images as drawing backgrounds

These permissions are only requested when you use related features, and you can revoke them at any time in system settings.

4. Third-Party Services

The App does NOT integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services. The App is a completely independent, locally-running drawing tool.

5. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • Please regularly backup important works to avoid data loss due to device damage or loss
  • Uninstalling the app will delete all local data
  • We recommend using device lock screen features to protect your privacy

6. Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for users of all ages. We do not actively collect personal information from any users (including children).

7. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be notified within the app. Continued use of the App indicates your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
