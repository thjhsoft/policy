App Overview

Draw Replay is a powerful drawing recording app that not only lets you create freely, but also records and replays your entire drawing process, and exports it as a video to share with friends. All data is stored completely locally to protect your privacy.

Detailed Instructions

1. Basic Drawing

  • Start Drawing: Slide your finger on the canvas to start drawing
  • Change Color: Tap the colored circle button in bottom-left, select preset colors or use color wheel
  • Adjust Brush: Tap brush icon, slide to adjust brush thickness (1-50px)
  • Select Style: Tap style icon, choose solid/dashed/dotted/dash-dot line

2. Canvas Operations

  • Zoom Canvas: Pinch/spread two fingers to zoom canvas (0.5x - 5x)
  • Move Canvas: Drag with two fingers to pan canvas position
  • Reset View: Tap reset button in top-right to restore default view
  • Undo/Redo: Tap undo/redo buttons in toolbar
  • Clear Canvas: Tap delete icon to clear all content

3. Recording Drawing Process

  • Start Recording: Tap "Record" button (red dot icon), button turns red when recording
  • Recording Indicator: During recording, every stroke is captured
  • Stop Recording: Tap "Stop" button again to end recording
  • Play Recording: Tap "Play" button to watch drawing process playback

üí° Tip: It's recommended to clear the canvas before recording, so the recorded video starts from a blank canvas for better results!

4. Playback Control

  • Play/Pause: During playback, tap "Pause" to pause, tap "Resume" to continue
  • Progress Adjustment: Drag progress bar at bottom of screen to jump to any position
  • Stop Playback: Tap "Stop" button to end playback, keeping current frame

5. Setting Background

  • Tap top menu ‚Üí "Background"
  • Solid Color: Select preset colors (white, black, gray, etc.)
  • Custom Image: Choose photo from gallery as background

6. Save & History

  • Save Work: Tap "Save" icon at top, work will be saved to history
  • View History: Tap "History" icon to browse all saved works
  • Load Work: Tap thumbnail in history list to load work and continue editing
  • Delete Work: Tap delete button in top-right of thumbnail

7. Export & Share

  • Tap top menu (three dots) ‚Üí Select function:
    • Save Image: Save current canvas as image to gallery
    • Share Image: Share current canvas via system share menu
    • Export Video: Export recorded drawing process as MP4 video (requires prior recording)

Tips & Tricks

  • Smooth Drawing: Use moderate brush thickness (5-15px) and lighter colors for more natural effects
  • Fine Control: Zoom in canvas (spread two fingers) to draw finer details
  • Layer Effect: Use semi-transparent colors with overlapping strokes to create layer effects
  • Background Match: Choose appropriate background color to make artwork stand out
  • Regular Saves: Remember to save important works timely to avoid accidental loss

FAQ

Q: Why does video export fail?

A: Please ensure: 1) Storage permission granted; 2) Drawing process recorded; 3) Sufficient device storage space.

Q: Can I edit saved works?

A: Yes! Tap the work thumbnail in history to load and continue editing.

Q: Does recording affect drawing performance?

A: Recording has minimal impact on performance, feel free to use it.

Q: Is data uploaded to cloud?

A: No. All data is stored completely locally on your device. We do not collect or upload any information.

Q: Will data be lost after uninstalling?

A: Yes. Uninstalling the app will delete all local data. Please export important works before uninstalling.
