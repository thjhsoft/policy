Account and Data Deletion

At FreshMap, we value your privacy. Since our app uses Google Play Games Services to manage leaderboards and Google OAuth for authentication, your data is primarily managed through your Google Account.

How to delete your leaderboard data and account link:

You can request the deletion of your game data and disconnect your Google account from FreshMap by following these steps on your Android device:

  1. Open the Settings app on your device.
  2. Tap on Google.
  3. Select Settings for Google Apps.
  4. Tap on Play Games.
  5. Select Delete Play Games account & data.
  6. Under "Delete individual game data," find FreshMap and tap Delete.

Types of data deleted:

  • Your leaderboard scores and rankings.
  • The association between your Google Account and FreshMap.
  • Any ephemeral game progress linked to your Play Games profile.
