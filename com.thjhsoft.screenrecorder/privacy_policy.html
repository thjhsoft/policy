🔒 Privacy Policy

Screen Recorder  ·  Effective Date: March 10, 2026

📋 Overview

Thank you for using Screen Recorder developed by thjhsoft. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

By using this app, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

📦 Information We Collect

Screen Recorder is designed to minimize data collection. Specifically:

  • Screen recordings & audio – All recordings are saved locally on your device. We never upload or access your recordings.
  • Camera data – Camera preview is rendered locally and is never transmitted externally.
  • App preferences – Settings such as resolution, audio options, and theme are stored locally using Android SharedPreferences.
  • Advertising data – We use Google AdMob to display ads that help fund continued app development and updates (see below).

📢 Advertising (Google AdMob)

This app contains advertisements served by Google AdMob. Advertising revenue directly supports the developer in maintaining, improving, and adding new features to Screen Recorder — at no cost to you.

Google AdMob may collect and use the following data to show you relevant ads:

  • Advertising ID (Android Ad ID)
  • Approximate location (country/region level)
  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • App usage signals
AdMob data collection is governed by Google's Privacy Policy. You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings under Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization.

🔐 Permissions Used

The app requests the following permissions and uses them solely for their stated purpose:

  • RECORD_AUDIO – Capture microphone input during recording.
  • CAMERA – Display a live camera preview overlay.
  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE / MEDIA_PROJECTION – Run the screen recorder as a foreground service.
  • SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW – Show the floating stop/camera button over other apps.
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS – Show recording status in the notification bar (Android 13+).

No permission data is shared with third parties except as described under Advertising above.

🌐 Third-Party SDKs

  • Google AdMob – Advertising. Privacy Policy
  • Google Play Services – Core Android services required by AdMob.

We do not integrate any analytics, crash reporting, or social SDKs beyond those listed above.

👶 Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

♻️ Data Retention & Deletion

All recordings and preferences are stored locally on your device. You may delete recordings directly from the app's Videos tab, or uninstall the app to remove all associated data. We hold no data on external servers.

📝 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the effective date at the top. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
