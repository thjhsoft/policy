🫧 BubbleTopia – Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 06, 2026

Welcome to BubbleTopia. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you play our game.

1. Information We Collect

BubbleTopia is designed to be a privacy-first game. We do not collect personal identifiers such as your name, email, or phone number. The following data may be collected:

  • Gameplay Data: Your high scores, current level, and game settings are stored locally on your device.
  • Device Information: Non-identifiable data such as device model, OS version, and language settings to ensure game compatibility.
  • Advertising IDs: Third-party advertising partners (Google AdMob) may collect your device's Advertising ID to serve relevant ads.

2. How We Use Information

Information collected through AdMob is used solely for the purpose of delivering relevant advertisements. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

3. Google AdMob & Advertising

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements within the app. Google may use the information collected to personalize ads shown to you. You can opt out of personalized advertising at any time through your device settings (Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization).

For more information, please review Google's Privacy Policy.

4. Children's Privacy

BubbleTopia is designed for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information, please contact us.

5. Data Retention

Game progress and settings are stored locally on your device using Android's SharedPreferences. This data never leaves your device and can be cleared by uninstalling the app.

6. Your Rights (GDPR / EEA Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for personalized advertising at any time

You can manage your consent preferences at any time via the Privacy Settings option in the app.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by the updated date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

8. Contact Us
